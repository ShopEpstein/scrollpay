How it works

Who sets the price?

You do. Enter any price in sats per XP when creating your listing. The order book sorts cheapest first — so competitive pricing gets filled faster.

Who buys the XP?

Advertisers running campaigns on ScrollPay use XP as their ad budget. They browse the order book and buy at prices they find fair. You negotiate nothing — just list and wait.

How do I get paid?

Once an advertiser purchases your listing, we send sats to the Bitcoin or Lightning address you provided. Lightning payments arrive in seconds.

Can I cancel my listing?

Yes — email support@scrollpay.app with your referral code and we'll cancel any open listing. Self-serve cancellation is coming soon.

Is there a minimum?

100 XP minimum per listing, 1 sat minimum per XP. You can only have one open listing at a time.
