How it works

How do I get paid?

Once your listing is matched, we send sats to the Bitcoin or Lightning address you provided. Lightning payments arrive in seconds. On-chain payments confirm within an hour.

How long does it take?

We process listings within 24–48 hours. You'll receive an email confirmation once your XP has been exchanged and the sats have been sent.

What is the current rate?

1 XP = 10 sats. At $100,000 BTC that's $0.01 per XP. The rate may change as the market matures — your listing locks in today's rate at the time of submission.

Who buys the XP?

Advertisers running campaigns on ScrollPay purchase XP to fund their ad budgets. Every XP you sell goes directly into an active campaign — so your attention keeps powering the cycle.

Is there a minimum?

Yes — 100 XP minimum per listing. You can only have one open listing at a time.
