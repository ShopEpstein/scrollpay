Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

TL;DR — We never read your page content, track your browsing history, or sell your data. We only know which domain you are on, whether you saw an ad for 2+ seconds, and whether you clicked it.

1. Who We Are

ScrollPay ("we", "us", or "our") operates the ScrollPay Chrome extension (Extension ID: fgdofdfagpjbbmkcgjmnfidokeopdcip) and the website at scrollpay.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect personal data when you use our extension or website.

For questions or requests related to this policy, contact us at privacy@scrollpay.app.

2. What Data We Collect and Why

The table below describes all personal and sensitive data we collect, the purpose for collecting it, and the legal basis where applicable.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We are designed to collect the minimum data necessary. We do not collect:

  • Page content, article text, form inputs, or anything you type
  • Full URLs, query strings, or search terms
  • Browsing history across sessions
  • Passwords, payment card numbers, or other financial credentials
  • Device fingerprints or hardware identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any data from pages where the extension detects a login form or banking interface

The extension's content script runs on all URLs (<all_urls> permission) solely to inject the ad widget overlay. It does not read, transmit, or store any page content.

4. Passwords and Sensitive Credentials — Explicit Statement

ScrollPay does not collect, access, read, store, or transmit passwords or any other sensitive credentials under any circumstances.

Although the extension's content script is granted permission to run on all URLs (including pages that contain login forms), this permission is used solely to inject the ad widget visual overlay into the page. The content script:

  • Does not read, intercept, or monitor any form fields, including password inputs.
  • Does not listen to keyboard events or capture keystrokes.
  • Does not access the DOM for any purpose other than appending the widget element.
  • Does not transmit any data from the visited page to our servers — only a boolean impression/click signal and the domain name are sent.

No password, authentication token, session cookie, or financial credential is ever collected, stored, or shared by ScrollPay. If you have any concerns about this, you may inspect the extension's source code directly in Chrome by navigating to chrome://extensions, enabling Developer Mode, and clicking "Inspect views".

5. How We Handle and Use Your Data

  • Impression and click signals are sent to our servers over encrypted HTTPS connections and used only to update your XP balance and generate aggregate advertiser reports.
  • Your email address is used only for the purposes you consented to at sign-up: account linking, winner notifications, and transactional emails.
  • We do not use your data for profiling, interest-based advertising, or automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects.
  • We apply a daily XP cap (5,000 XP) to prevent abuse and maintain fair reward distribution.

6. Data Storage

  • Cloud storage: Account data (email, XP balance, transaction history) is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, hosted in the United States. Firebase is subject to Google's security and compliance standards. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Local storage: Your user ID and session state are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. This data never leaves your device except as part of authenticated API requests to our servers.
  • We do not use browser cookies or localStorage.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Advertisers: We share aggregated, non-personal impression and click counts so advertisers can measure campaign performance. No personal data (name, email, user ID) is included.
  • Service providers: We use Google Firebase (data storage and authentication) and may use other processors who act on our behalf under data processing agreements. These providers are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required to do so by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of ScrollPay or others.
  • Business transfers: If ScrollPay is acquired or merges, your data may transfer to the new entity, which will be bound by this policy.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g. financial transaction records, which may be retained for up to 7 years). Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely as it cannot identify you.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Firebase Firestore data at rest is encrypted by Google.
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Firestore security rules enforce per-user data access — you can only read and write your own records.

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at privacy@scrollpay.app.

10. Third-Party Services

The extension integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Authentication) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Fonts (Inter typeface, loaded from fonts.googleapis.com on the privacy and onboarding pages)

We do not embed third-party analytics SDKs (e.g. Google Analytics, Mixpanel) in the extension.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored and processed primarily in the United States via Google Firebase. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, your data may be transferred to and processed in a country with different data protection laws. Such transfers rely on Google's Standard Contractual Clauses or other appropriate safeguards.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing relies on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Opt out of sale/sharing (CCPA/CPRA): We do not sell or share personal data, so no opt-out is required, but you may contact us to confirm.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@scrollpay.app. We will respond within 30 days. We may verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

You may also uninstall the extension at any time to immediately stop all data collection by the extension.
