How ScrollPay Works

A closed-loop economy where ad spend flows directly back to users and holders.

01

What is XP?

XP is ScrollPay's advertising credit. You earn XP free by viewing ads while you browse.

XP can only be used to fund ad campaigns on ScrollPay. XP has no cash value and is not a cryptocurrency.

Advertisers can trade unused XP credits peer-to-peer.
