What is XP?

XP is ScrollPay's free attention credit. You earn it automatically by viewing ads while you browse with the extension installed. Nothing to buy, nothing to stake.

XP has no cash value and is not a financial instrument or cryptocurrency. It is an ad-viewing credit that can be used to enter the weekly Bitcoin raffle or listed for sale to other users on the open market at whatever price the market decides.

Mining XP costs you nothing. Your time and attention are already yours — we just pay you for them.
