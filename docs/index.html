Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. ScrollPay is free to install and use. You earn XP just by browsing with it on.

How do I win a prize?

XP piles up nonstop while you scroll. Each week we draw a winner from the XP pool and pay out in Bitcoin. The more XP you've stacked, the better your odds. Or skip the draw entirely and sell your XP directly on the XP market.

What are prizes paid in?

Bitcoin (BTC). We send the weekly prize directly to the winner's Bitcoin wallet — no conversion, no gift cards, no middleman. If you don't have a wallet yet, any free app like Coinbase or Cash App lets you receive BTC instantly.

Can I sell or swap my XP?

Yes. XP is the currency advertisers use to run campaigns on ScrollPay. If you'd rather cash out now than wait for the weekly draw, you can sell your XP directly to advertisers or swap it through the platform. Your attention earned it — spend it how you want.

What data do you collect?

Only what's needed to count ad views and run the draw: the domain you're on (e.g. "twitter.com"), whether an ad was visible, whether you tapped it, and an optional email so we can reach you if you win. Never page content, URLs, or anything you type. See our privacy policy.

Which sites does it work on?

Everywhere — Twitter/X, Reddit, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and any other site you visit.
