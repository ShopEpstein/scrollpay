Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

⚡ TL;DR — We never read your page content, track your browsing history, or sell your data. We only know: which domain you're on, whether you saw an ad for 2+ seconds, and whether you clicked it.

What We Collect

  • Domain name only — e.g. "twitter.com". Not URLs, not page content, not query strings.
  • Ad impressions — whether the ad widget was visible for 2+ seconds.
  • Ad clicks — whether you clicked the CTA button.
  • Your email (optional) — to associate your XP across devices and to contact you if you win a prize draw.
  • Referral code (optional) — to credit the friend who referred you.

What We Never Collect

  • Page content, article text, or anything you type
  • Full URLs or search queries
  • Browsing history across sessions
  • Passwords, credit card numbers, or any sensitive input
  • Device fingerprints or persistent identifiers beyond your user ID

How We Use Your Data

  • To count impressions and award XP to your account
  • To calculate your entries in the prize draw and contact winners
  • To prevent abuse (daily cap of 5,000 XP)
  • To credit referrals

Data Storage

Your balance and account data are stored in Google Firebase Firestore. Your user ID and session state are stored locally in chrome.storage.local on your device. We do not use cookies or localStorage.

Data Sharing

We share impression counts (not personal data) with advertisers so they can see how many people viewed their ads. We never sell your email or any personal information to third parties.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@scrollpay.app.

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Uninstall the extension at any time to stop all data collection

Contact

Questions? Email privacy@scrollpay.app
